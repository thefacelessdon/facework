# Face.works — Purpose

Locked strategic direction. These premises are load-bearing — if any
becomes false, the system changes.

## Premises

1. **Creator-first wedge.** Face.works works with individual creators and
   cultural brands with their own audience and identity, not with
   platforms or aggregators. The wedge is creator-owned commerce
   infrastructure.

2. **GAMUT counts only if audited.** The GAMUT practice is meaningful
   only when it can be audited against the open Facework Protocol. An
   un-audited engagement is not a GAMUT engagement.

3. **Pricing philosophy first.** Pricing reflects what the engagement is
   worth to the creator, not the agency's labor cost. Per-engagement flat
   rate, not hourly. Discount only when the founder has a stake in
   amplifying the creator's signal.

4. **Per-studio flat rate.** Studio engagements are priced flat per
   studio, not per project, not per seat. This keeps the economics
   honest at the studio scale.

5. **Studios at Beta only.** Studio engagements are accepted only when
   Face.works is past MVP and into Beta with reliable delivery. MVP-stage
   Face.works does individual creator work only.

6. **Identity shift at Beta.** Face.works' positioning changes at Beta —
   from "founder-as-practitioner" to "practice-as-infrastructure." This
   is a planned shift, not a brand pivot.

7. **Revenue floor of $5,000 per engagement.** Below this, the engagement
   does not justify the protocol's full depth. Lower-cost engagements use
   a Thesis-evidence-level protocol run with reduced artifact production.

8. **Clock starts at first payment.** Engagement timelines are measured
   from first payment, not from signed proposal. Pre-payment work is
   sales activity, not delivery.

9. **Capacity calibration per quarter.** Face.works delivers no more than
   the declared per-quarter capacity. Over-commitment compounds entropy
   in delivery quality.

10. **No-go lines (locked).** Face.works does not work with:
    - Projects whose audience is hypothetical (no named users, no
      observed behavior).
    - Platforms or aggregators that extract from the creators using
      them.
    - Engagements that require violating the TasteContract or the
      Facework Protocol's sovereignty principles.

11. **Certification at scale only.** Practitioner certification (other
    studios using GAMUT) ships only after Face.works delivers ≥3 audited
    engagements with public proof artifacts. Premature certification
    dilutes the practice.

12. **New primitives must be tested.** Additions to the Facework
    Protocol made by Face.works ship only after being tested on a
    real engagement. Speculative additions are deferred.

## Stage gate criteria

Per PROTOCOL.md §8:

**MVP exit (current stage):**
- A real creator completes the engagement end-to-end.
- The engagement produces repeatable value above $5,000.
- Stage assumptions documented with pass/fail evidence.

**Beta entry:**
- MVP exit proof is complete and reproducible.
- Workflow playbooks run without founder-only context.
- Critical dependencies and ownership boundaries explicit.

## Wedge details

- **Audience:** Individual creators with active communities (≥1k engaged
  followers AND ≥1 published paid offer)
- **Offer:** GAMUT-delivered Facework Protocol engagement producing
  owned infrastructure + handoff package
- **Channel:** Inbound from creator community + founder network
- **Economic logic:** Flat per-engagement rate ≥$5k, capacity-bound, no
  hourly billing
