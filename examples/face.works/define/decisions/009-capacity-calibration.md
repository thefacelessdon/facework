# Decision 009: Capacity Requires Calibration After First Runs

## The Question
What is the realistic solo + agent engagement capacity per month?

## Resolution
Plan conservatively at ~2 engagements/month. Actual capacity will be
calibrated after the first 3 engagement runs, accounting for GTM,
prospect qualification, closure work, and operational overhead.

## Alternatives Considered
- Lock at 2 Full Protocol/month: Rejected — too early to know. The
  agent stack may be faster or slower than estimated.
- Keep 2-4 range: Rejected — the range was too wide and created
  inconsistencies across documents. 4 Full Protocols/month is not
  realistic with GTM overhead.

## Reasoning
The 5-8 day engagement estimate comes from GAMUT (4 days), but GAMUT
was a self-directed build, not a client engagement with review sessions.
Client engagements will have scheduling overhead. Need real data.

## Implications
- Revenue projections use ~2 engagements/month as planning assumption
- Capacity number will be updated after engagement runs 1-3
- If actual capacity is higher, revenue ceiling increases
- If lower, pricing or scope may need adjustment

## Coherence Impact
Increases coherence. Using estimates instead of inventing certainty
is what the protocol demands of its own practitioners.
