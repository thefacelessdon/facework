# Decision 012: Studios Removed from MVP Scope

## The Question
Are studios/agencies a valid MVP customer segment or Beta-only?

## Resolution
Studios are Beta-only. Removed "MVP Secondary" label from business model.
Studios are documented for forward planning but not commercially pursued
during MVP. If a studio approaches, log interest for Beta; they can hire
Facework for a direct creator engagement but cannot license the methodology.

## Reasoning
Decision 002 (creator-first wedge) already established this direction.
The business model's "MVP Secondary" label contradicted that decision.
Alignment restored.

## Implications
- Business model updated: studios labeled "Beta — not MVP"
- GTM playbook targets creators only
- Studio interest during MVP is logged, not pursued
