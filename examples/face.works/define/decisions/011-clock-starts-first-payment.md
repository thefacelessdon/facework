# Decision 011: 12-Month Clock Starts at First Payment Received

## The Question
When does the 12-month no-go line clock begin?

## Resolution
The clock starts when the first client payment is received. Not contract
signed (could be delayed), not engagement completed (too late), not today
(building the protocol infrastructure is pre-revenue work).

## Reasoning
Payment received is the clearest, most honest trigger. It marks the
moment the commercial model is live and being tested by the market.
Everything before that is preparation.

## Implications
- Clock is NOT running yet (no payments received)
- First payment = month 0 of the 12-month window
- All no-go line documents updated to specify "first payment received"
