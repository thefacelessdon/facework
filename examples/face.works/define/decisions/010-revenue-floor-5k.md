# Decision 010: Revenue Hard Floor is $5K/month

## The Question
Is the hard no-go trigger at $5K or $10K monthly revenue?

## Resolution
$5K/month is the hard no-go line. Below $5K for 3+ consecutive months
after 12 months from first payment = restructure the commercial model.
$10K/month is the sustainability target, not the kill trigger.

## Reasoning
$5K is the minimum to cover costs. $10K is comfortable. Setting the
kill trigger at $10K would force premature restructuring during a
natural ramp period. $5K gives runway while maintaining accountability.

## Implications
- $5K–$10K/month = concerning but not fatal (soft investigation)
- Below $5K for 3+ months after 12 months = hard restructure trigger
- All documents updated to use consistent language
