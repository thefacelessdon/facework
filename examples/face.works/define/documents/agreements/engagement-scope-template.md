## What's Included

### Full Protocol

| Phase | What You Get | Your Time |
|-------|-------------|-----------|
| Semantics | Signal thesis, canonical language, distortion risks | 1 session (30 min) |
| Field | Audience field map, trust transfer path, adoption dynamics | 1 session (30 min) |
| Taste | Taste contract + DESIGN.md (typography, color, grid, motion, tone) | 1 session (30-60 min) |
| Frequency | Business model, ownership structure, exit guarantee, extraction check | 1 session (30-60 min) |
| Current | Strategic decisions locked with reasoning, ownership dilemma resolved | 1-2 sessions (30-60 min each) |
| Flow | Operational playbooks for every workflow, agent/human ownership mapping | 1 session (30-60 min) |
| Stability | Architecture specs per system, data models, edge cases, acceptance criteria | 1 session (30-60 min) |
| Resonance | Working prototype with demo mode, typed schema, test suite, design applied | 1 session (30-60 min) |
| Integrity | Pressure test, sovereignty audit, cross-layer alignment check | — (no session needed) |
| Coherence | README, review brief, project tracker, engineering guide, clean repo | 1 session (30 min walkthrough) |

**Total creator time:** 8-10 sessions, ~6-8 hours across the engagement.
**Everything between sessions is handled by the agent + operator.**

### Foundation Only

Phases included: Semantics, Field, Taste, Frequency, Current, Flow, Stability.
No prototype, no integrity review, no engineering guide.

**Total creator time:** 5-7 sessions, ~4-5 hours.

## What's NOT Included

- Production code (the prototype is a reference implementation, not the final build)
- Ongoing operations (playbooks document how to operate; Facework doesn't operate for you)
- Hiring or team management
- Domain registration, hosting, or infrastructure provisioning
- Legal review of agreements (we produce templates; your attorney reviews them)
- Marketing execution (playbooks define the strategy; you or your team execute)

## How It Works

1. **You sign this scope + agreement.** First payment received.
2. **We schedule the first session.** Intake conversation (30-60 min).
3. **The protocol runs.** Between sessions, the agent generates artifacts.
   You review and steer in sessions. You make the decisions.
4. **Handoff.** Everything transferred to your accounts. Walkthrough session.
   A new builder can start Day 1 without a meeting.

## What Makes This Different

- **You own everything.** Not licensed. Not hosted on our platform. Yours.
- **Open formats.** Markdown, Git, JSON. No proprietary tools required.
- **The protocol is open.** You can read the full spec at face.works/protocol.
  You could run it yourself. You're paying for speed, taste, and domain expertise.
- **Extraction check built in.** Phase 1 verifies your business model doesn't
  extract from the community it serves. If it does, we surface it — and either
  fix it together or decline the engagement.
- **Clean exit.** If you stop mid-engagement, you keep everything produced to date.
  No transition fees. No retention tactics.
