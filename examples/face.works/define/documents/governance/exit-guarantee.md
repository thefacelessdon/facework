# Facework Protocol — Exit Guarantee

Version: 1.0
Date: 2026-03-23

---

## Principle

A creator or brand who ends their relationship with Facework leaves with
everything they brought and everything that was built. Exit is clean, not
punitive. This is the single clearest signal of whether a system extracts
or sustains.

---

## What You Take With You

### From a Build Engagement:

1. **All artifacts produced** — governance documents, specs, architecture,
   prototypes, playbooks, decision records, handoff packages. Every file,
   every document, every diagram. No exceptions.

2. **All code** — prototype code, schemas, test suites, configuration.
   Delivered in a clean repository you own.

3. **All data** — any data created, collected, or processed during the
   engagement belongs to you.

4. **Your brand and identity** — Facework never claims credit for your
   brand, aesthetic, cultural position, or creative output.

5. **Your relationships** — suppliers, partners, collaborators, customers
   introduced or identified during the engagement are yours.

### From a Licensing Arrangement (Studios):

1. **Your client work** — everything you produced for clients using the
   methodology belongs to you and your clients.

2. **Your process adaptations** — workflow customizations you developed
   on top of the methodology are yours.

3. **Your client relationships** — Facework never contacts your clients.

4. **You lose:** Access to the licensed agent skills (/fw-* tooling) and
   the right to certify new work as protocol-conformant. Existing
   certifications remain valid.

---

## What Facework Retains

1. **The protocol itself** — Facework Protocol remains open and owned by
   Facework. Your use of it does not grant ownership of the standard.

2. **The right to reference** — Facework may reference the engagement
   (that a project was built using the protocol) unless you opt out.
   Facework will never share details, artifacts, or proprietary information.

3. **Methodology learnings** — general patterns learned during an
   engagement may inform methodology improvements. No proprietary details
   are ever incorporated.

---

## Format

All deliverables are provided in **open, human-readable formats**:
- Markdown documents (no proprietary doc formats)
- Standard code repositories (Git)
- JSON/YAML for structured data
- PNG/SVG for diagrams

No deliverable requires Facework software, accounts, or services to access.

---

## Timing

Exit is immediate upon request. No waiting period. No "transition phase"
that creates dependency. Final deliverables are transferred within 5
business days of exit request.

---

## The Test

> If a creator built their system through Facework and Facework disappeared
> tomorrow, the creator's system would continue to function, their
> documentation would remain readable, their code would remain runnable,
> and their business would continue operating.

If this test fails at any point, the exit guarantee has been violated and
the protocol is in breach of its own governing truth.
