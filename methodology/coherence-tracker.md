## Scoring Reference

The governing equation: `Coherence = (Flow × Resonance) / (1 + Entropy)`

Each variable scored 1-5. See `/fw-diagnostic` for the full rubric.

- **Flow:** How easily energy moves through the system (1=constant friction, 5=invisible)
- **Resonance:** How deeply the system connects with its community (1=generic, 5="that's for me")
- **Entropy:** How much structural weakness and extraction remains (1=critical, 5=self-sustaining)

Coherence score = (F × R) / (1 + E). Range: 0.17 (worst) to 12.5 (best).
