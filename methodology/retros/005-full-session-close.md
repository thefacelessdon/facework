## What the next project must do differently

1. **Start at Phase 1 (Semantics), not Phase 4 (Frequency).** The reconciled
   sequence begins with meaning extraction. The first three phases (Semantics,
   Field, Taste) set the foundation that Frequency and Current build on.

2. **Run the full 10-phase sequence end-to-end.** The reconciled structure
   has never been tested as a complete sequence on a real engagement. The
   self-application tested phases individually and retroactively. A real
   engagement proves or disproves the sequence.

3. **Score from evidence, not from the user.** The coherence score is derived
   from what happened, not from how the builder feels about it. Flow is
   measured by whether participants hit friction. Resonance is measured by
   community response. Structural Integrity is measured by contradiction count.
