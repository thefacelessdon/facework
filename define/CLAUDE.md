## What Facework Is

An open protocol for turning cultural signal into coherent, ownable business
systems — with commercial services and tooling on top.

## Current Stage: MVP (Services Phase)

You run the protocol directly for creators/cultural brands. No licensing,
no certification, no studio partnerships yet.

## Business Model (MVP)

- **Revenue:** Build services for creators/cultural brands ($10K–$25K per engagement)
- **Hard floor:** $5K/month (no-go trigger)
- **Target:** $10K/month
- **Capacity:** ~2 engagements/month (solo + agent stack, calibrate after first 3 runs)
- **MVP exit:** Compound gate — $5K+/month for 3 consecutive months AND 3 completed
  protocol runs with documented outcomes

## Stage Boundaries

- **MVP → Beta:** Revenue floor met + 3 protocol runs proven. Identity shifts from
  "you running it" to "the protocol others run."
- **Beta → Scale:** Studio successfully runs protocol under license. Certification
  launches with 5-project minimum.

## No-Go Lines

- 12 months from first payment received, below $5K/month → restructure
- 6 months no engagement → reassess go-to-market
- Non-negotiable violated → stop and fix

## Non-Negotiables

1. Protocol stays open
2. Creator sovereignty is absolute — they own all output
3. No lock-in — open formats, no proprietary dependencies
4. Conduits, not containers — Facework never controls creator infrastructure
5. Energy transfer within 12 months
