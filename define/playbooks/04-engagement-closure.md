# Engagement Closure & Case Study Playbook

How a completed engagement becomes proof and feeds the portfolio.

**Operating model:** Conformance auditing is agent-native — the agent
runs the checklist. The human validates judgment calls and handles
the creator relationship.

## Trigger

Engagement delivery complete — deliverables transferred, creator
confirms navigability, final payment received.

## Steps

### 1. Conformance Audit (Agent runs → Human validates — 30 min)

**Agent:** Run the conformance model against the completed engagement:
- Phase-by-phase gate check (automated — checklist against artifacts)
- Extraction check (structured — agent fills template, human validates)
- Cross-reference check (automated — agent verifies number consistency)
- Coherence test (human validates — requires judgment)

**Agent assigns conformance level. Human confirms or overrides.**

The extraction check and coherence test are the two steps that require
human judgment. The rest is structural verification the agent does better
than a human (it won't miss a cross-reference inconsistency).

### 2. Internal Debrief (Human — 15 min, agent records)

While it's fresh:
- What worked? What was harder than expected?
- Where did the methodology gap?
- Phase timing: actual vs. estimated?
- Domain-specific blind spots?

**Agent records and files.** This feeds into /fw-diagnostic.

### 3. Case Study (Human — relationship; Agent — production)

**Human asks:** "Would you be willing to be referenced?"
Three levels: anonymous, named, full case study. No pressure.

**If yes → Agent drafts the case study** from engagement artifacts:
- Who, problem, process, output, outcome, sovereignty proof
- Creator reviews and approves before publication

### 4. Update Tracking (Agent — automated)

Agent updates proof tracking, artifact registry status, revenue log,
and flags any methodology gaps for future /fw-diagnostic.

## Ownership (Agent-Native)

| Step | Agent Does | Human Does |
|------|-----------|------------|
| Conformance audit | Runs checklist, cross-references, scores | Validates extraction + coherence judgment |
| Debrief | Records and files | Reflects honestly |
| Case study ask | — | Relationship |
| Case study production | Drafts from artifacts | Reviews tone |
| Tracking | Updates all tables | — |

## Energy Flow Check

Creator controls their story. Agent produces the case study from their
own artifacts — no invention, no spin. Creator approves before publication.

## Completion

Audit documented. Debrief filed. Case study status recorded.
Tracking updated. 45-60 minutes total.
