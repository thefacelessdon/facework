# Decision 004: No-Go Lines

## The Question
What conditions force Facework to stop, pivot, or fundamentally restructure?

## Resolution
**Primary no-go line:** If after 12 months from the first paid engagement,
monthly revenue has not consistently reached $5K/month, the commercial model
requires fundamental restructuring. The protocol stays open, but the business
layer needs a different approach.

## No-Go Line Specifics

**Hard kill triggers:**
- 12 months from first engagement, revenue below $5K/month for 3+ consecutive
  months → restructure the commercial model
- A non-negotiable is violated and cannot be restored → stop and fix before
  any new work
- The author's energy is the sole source of protocol credibility after 12
  months (no creator-driven proof exists) → extraction pattern, must fix

**Soft pivot triggers (investigate, don't kill):**
- 6 months with no paid engagement → reassess go-to-market, not the protocol
- First 3 engagements produce no referrals → output quality or market fit issue
- A creator engagement produces a system that fails the extraction check →
  methodology gap, not a business model issue

## Alternatives Considered
- 6 months / no engagement: Considered as a hard line but rejected — 6 months
  is too short for a protocol sale. Made it a soft trigger instead.
- 3 engagements / no referral: Considered as a hard line but rejected — early
  engagements may be under NDA or in stealth. Made it a soft trigger.

## Reasoning
The 12-month revenue floor is the binding constraint because it maps directly
to the energy transfer deadline from the extraction check. If the protocol
can't sustain its own operator, it's extracting from them. The soft triggers
create earlier intervention points without premature panic.

## Implications
- Unblocks: Freedom to invest fully for 12 months with a defined exit
- Constrains: Must track monthly revenue and proof milestones explicitly
- Calendar: Clock starts when the first paid engagement closes (not today)

## Coherence Impact
High coherence. A protocol about preventing extraction must have extraction
checks on itself. The no-go lines are the self-diagnostic mechanism.
