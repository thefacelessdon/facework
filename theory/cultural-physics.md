# Cultural Physics

## Why It Exists

Cultural systems often follow extraction dynamics:
communities generate energy, institutions capture value, and source communities
retain little infrastructure control. Product systems that ignore this pattern
recreate it by default.

Facework treats this as an architectural problem, not a branding problem.

## Governing Questions

1. Who generates the energy?
2. Who controls the conduit?
3. Who captures upside?
4. What is portable on exit?
5. What prevents extraction under scale pressure?

## Working Model

Coherence rises when ownership, behavior, and narrative align.

`Coherence = (Flow * Resonance) / (1 + Entropy)`

- **Flow**: adaptive behavior and operational integrity.
- **Resonance**: transmission quality across real audiences.
- **Entropy**: contradictions, drift, brittleness, and extraction pressures.

## Design Implications

- Governance must be explicit before implementation.
- Canonical numbers and ownership terms must be enforceable in architecture.
- Taste is a system-level quality function, not a cosmetic layer.
- Exit must preserve participant agency or the system is extractive.

## Validation Criteria

A culturally coherent system should pass:
- **signal test**: intent is legible to source communities,
- **ownership test**: rights and upside match contribution,
- **behavior test**: operations reinforce declared values,
- **handoff test**: others can operate the system without diluting its identity.
