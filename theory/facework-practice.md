# Facework Practice

Facework is a coherence practice for turning intuition into infrastructure.

## Core Thesis

Most teams fail from contradiction, not effort. They ship surfaces before locking
governance, language, ownership, and architectural truth. Facework forces sequence:
signal -> direction -> structure -> behavior -> transmission -> stress -> integration.

This is not project management. It is identity architecture for products, systems,
and organizations.

## Primitive Stack

1. **Frequency** - extract irreducible signal and governing economics.
2. **Current** - resolve strategic dilemmas and lock direction.
3. **Stability** - specify architecture before implementation.
4. **Flow** - design operational behavior under real constraints.
5. **Resonance** - make the system transmissible through working artifacts.
6. **Entropy** - expose structural weakness before failure.
7. **Coherence** - integrate into a handoff-ready whole.
8. **Diagnostic** - evolve the practice from real execution evidence.

## Cultural Intelligence Layer

Facework extends technical rigor with cultural rigor:
- What signal are we amplifying?
- Whose energy powers this system?
- Who controls the infrastructure?
- What quality standard defines taste in this domain?
- Can this system sustain its source rather than extract from it?

If these are unresolved, scale multiplies contradiction.

## Output Standard

Every phase must produce:
- explicit decisions,
- canonical sources of truth,
- verifiable acceptance criteria,
- and downstream-ready artifacts another team can execute without tribal context.

## Method Principle

Build the architecture, and the identity becomes legible.
Ignore coherence, and the system will eventually contradict itself.
